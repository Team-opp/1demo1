⭐ Peer Review Scoring — 0 to 4
0
No Error
1
Minor
2
Moderate
3
Significant
4
Critical
0 — No error. Report complete and accurate. Case closes automatically.
1 — Minor omission with no clinical impact. Simple agreement closes the case.
2 — Moderate missed finding with potential significance. Written response required.
3 — Significant error that could affect patient management. Full written response required.
4 — Critical diagnostic error. Serious patient safety implications. Mandatory escalation.
💡 Key rule: Score 0–1 closes with a simple agreement. Score 2 or above requires your written explanation. If you believe the reviewer's assessment is wrong, escalate to Adjudication for an HR binding decision.
